a little about me

coder

I am a self-taught developer, who learned how to code with perseverance and hard work, striving to create a better future for myself by doing something I loved. Technology has been a lifelong interest of mine, and I've greatly enjoyed having the opportunity to be just a little more a part of that world.

It is now coming up to a year since I started my coding journey. And since then I have grown into a junior full stack developer, building efficient applications within quick turnarounds. I'm excited for what the future has in store.

prior experience

Before switching paths to web development, I completed my B.A. in English, planning on going into healthcare for my masters. As I was applying to programs, I began my professional career at La Sierra University where I realized how I greatly enjoyed working with and for students. Now I hope to take that interest to one day build applications for students of all ages.

Later when I began the program, I was working at Prime Healthcare's marketing department. I was lucky to be in a great team where I had the opportunity to be included in larger projects, being given more responsibilities than was accustomed for my role. On my ninth month of working there, I was awarded employee of the year.
